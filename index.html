Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

innovation

Bangladesh drives innovation through technology, startups, and digital platforms, improving productivity, economic inclusion, and citizens’ quality of life.

Sustainability

Renewable energy, green industries, and climate resilience initiatives help Bangladesh balance sustainable development with environmental preservation.

Projects like Padma Bridge and urban modernization enhance connectivity, foster trade, and support Bangladesh’s transition to a tech-driven economy.

Empowerment

Gender equality, microfinance, and skill development empower communities, fostering equity, resilience, and sustainable growth across Bangladesh.
